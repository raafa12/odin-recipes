Margarita pizza

This pizza gets its name from the Queen Margaret Theresa of Savoy, who asked the most famous chef in the city Raffaele Esposito to cook for her some pizzas and he baptized the one he liked the most with her name.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella Balls
  • Fresh Basil
  • OLive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible
  2. Extend the dough
  3. Put the tomato sauce
  4. Put the fresh mozzarella cheese into it
  5. Put some fresh basil leaves
  6. Get the pizza into the oven
  7. In 5 10 minutes take it out put some olive oil and is ready to eat
