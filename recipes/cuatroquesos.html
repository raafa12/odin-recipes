Quattro Formaggi Pizza

The choice of cheeses is not random, they should be full-fat or semi-fat and vary in flavour. Besides mozzarella, the quattro formaggi usually combines a blue or mature cheese, a soft cheese or a creamy cheese, and a hard cheese.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella cheese
  • Goat cheese
  • Gorgonzola cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature possible
  2. Extend the dough
  3. Put the tomato sauce
  4. Put the goat cheese
  5. Put the fresh mozzarella cheese
  6. Put parmesan cheese
  7. Get the pizza into the oven
  8. In 5 10 minutes take it out put some more olive oil and is ready to eat
